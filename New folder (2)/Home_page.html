The Institutions are governed by Balwant Educational Society
chaired by District and Sessions Judge, Agra as the President
and Raja Anirudh Pal Singh ji of the royal family of Awagarh as
the Vice President. The credit for the same goes to Late Raja
Balwant Singh ji, the erstwhile ruler of Awagarh Estate known
for his vision and commitment for the spread of education,
way back in 1885.

Hon'ble Raja Anirudh Pal Singh Ji
Vice President
Balwant Educational Society
I feel delighted to observe the steady growth and development of our institutions which are premier
educational institutions running under the umbrella of Balwant Educational Society. From the day of
establishment to this date, our institutions have blossomed into a well-structured seat of learning
and research in all areas of education. It is quite exhilarating that our institutions are an embodiment
of my long-cherished dream, of establishing a centre of excellence for providing world-class education
to the students drawn from different sections of society. The success of our group is not sheer luck but
but the hard work and dedication of the team. These institutions possess an extremely dedicated team
of faculty members which guide, mentor and counsel the students continuously and bring out the best
in them. I am sure that this journey of excellence would continue even at a faster pace in the coming
years and I am quite confident that our institutes will carry on climbing the ladder of success.
Wishing you all good luck and a very bright future ahead!
Hon'ble Yuvraj Ambareesh Pal Singh Ji
Secretary
Balwant Educational Society

Located in the heart of the city in a serene and eco-friendly environment with constantly expanding

confident that our mission of Social Empowerment through Quality Education will remain a reality
in the times to come. Finally, I congratulate the Head, Faculty Members, Staff and the students of our
institutions for the milestones of success they have already achieved and wish them luck for climbing
higher summits of achievements.
